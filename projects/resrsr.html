Professional Training System

5-Day Split Training Program

Beginner to Intermediate

This program is designed specifically for strongman training with minimal equipment. Each workout focuses on compound movements that build functional strength applicable to strongman events.

Program Benefits

  • Optimized for muscle hypertrophy and strength gains
  • Adaptable for home training with minimal equipment
  • Progressive overload system for continued growth

Equipment Needed

  • Heavy stones or concrete blocks (various weights)
  • Metal pipes or wooden logs
  • DIY sandbags (use old bags filled with sand)
  • Sturdy chairs or boxes for elevated exercises

Training Principles

  • Progressive overload: Increase weight/reps weekly
  • Time under tension: Control movements
